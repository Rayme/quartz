created: 2025-12-02

I spent the whole day working with Gemini 3 Pro at my job. I asked it to analyze the numbers and situation. It explained quite well when I provided enough data for it. I was surprised that it could solve some hard problems or offer actionable steps.

It brought me comfort. My perspective on my work shifted. With the help of AI, I looked into those numbers in another way. This helped clear my head too. I always say that AI may affect the way we think or we write, but if we use it carefully or with caution, the advantages outweigh the disadvantages.

Think from a different perspective, I paid the AI service and it pays off. This is persuasive. The important thing we need to do, besides using it carefully, is to ask _accurate and sufficient_ questions.

I'd like to share a marvelous essay with you, ["How To Ask Questions The Smart Way"](https://github.com/selfteaching/How-To-Ask-Questions-The-Smart-Way). It benefited me greatly from day one.
