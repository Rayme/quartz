When I first started this blog I have problem on coming up with a blog title/name. In the end I just decided to use my real name: Joseph Chan. No one will see this blog in a long time anyway. So I just don't care about it. And I won't change it for a while.

There are some reasons. I see a lot of blogs that just use the author's name. It doesn't matter when the author publishes nice contents. I may not be able to do that yet, but once I do, the blog name becomes a brand.

That reminds me of my WordPress blog. It uses my nickname. It has been a decade but there is no issue at all. People just call me by that name.

The Bearblog is awesome. So should the people using it.
