I have a WordPress blog (not anymore), a Quartz digital garden, a Bearblog and several note-taking software. The writing process may differ. I used to write in WordPress directly, then just hit publish. Then I found different ways. Such as starting to write in Obsidian, make some drafts, then decide whether publish or not.

Things are a bit different now. I may start writing in Anytype, make more drafts, edit them and then organize them in Obsidian, then publish them on blog or digital garden. Even writing directly in digital garden, then I found out it is a complete post that I can publish on blog. Contents flow between blogs and garden, sometimes Anytype and Bearblog.

It feels good to see contents flow between different places. Although I sometimes can not find the exact file I want, but I will eventually find it. Contents flow between big Internet companies in China. From xiaohongshu to douyin, from wechat to weibo, they just don't stop. I hate to see it. But I don't know why I hate it. Probably it is because of the evil they did.
