---
title: What Does The Dream Say?
created: 2025-12-04
---
I had this dream:

I walk on an inclined path, trees and pebble stones all along the way. The air is blue and transparent, an artificial lake is to the left of the path. The lake is surrounded by a long wooden fence, I can even smell the wood.

I keep walking. Then I see a large cement canteen, it has two floors. But I never go up to the second floor. People come and go, most of them are students. There are also office workers at my age, walking around rapidly. There is a giant metal bucket outside the canteen. People pick up food from the bucket and then go upstairs. The ceiling glass is light blue, which makes the canteen blue.

I am not hungry so I don't go in but keep going. I see another lake with a thin path on the lake. A small round island enclosed by a stone fence shows up in the middle of the lake. A couple is talking at close range on the island. I don't know them so I keep walking.

In the end, I walk into a house covered with glass. The room is filled with sunshine coming down from the ceiling. A long couch is placed against the wall. I lie on the couch and am about to take a nap. Then I hear noises coming close to me outside of the room. I don't know what that is but I sense danger. I sit up and take a defensive stance.

Then I wake up. I've had the same dream several times. The same path, canteen, two lakes, round island and the big glass house. I wrote my dreams down in my diary. I figured the dream appears in different years and on different dates.

I bet I will have the same dream soon. I don't have the answer yet, maybe never will.

Day 49.
