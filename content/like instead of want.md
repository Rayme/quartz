title: Focus on what I like, not what I want
---
[James Clear](https://jamesclear.com/3-2-1/october-3-2024)

> Wanting is the desire you feel before doing something. Liking is the satisfaction you feel after doing something. Let your likes guide you.

Do those things that I like. I like how it feels when I have written an article or taken a great photo. I like to show and post those nice photos that I took. I like how it feels to sit down and watch a great movie. I don't want to write something about it but to share my perspective of that movie. I like the feeling of having a digital garden online. I like the feeling of having [[gpd win max 2 review|a computer in the palm of my hand]].

I feel complete peace and satisfaction doing these things. Let it guide me.
