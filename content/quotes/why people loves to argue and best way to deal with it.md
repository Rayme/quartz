2021-08-24 [Nanette Litya](https://thelowdown.momentum.asia/why-people-loves-to-argue-and-best-way-to-deal-with-it)

Those who want to win an argument is because they are either being self-defense or getting attention from someone or trying to control something or get respect from someone. Maybe they are just narcissists. Either way, just ignore them.

To do that, first we could just stop arguing with them. Just **stop it**, and they'll just leave. End the conversation without agreeing with them. Most importantly, **don't get provoked**. Seriously, this one DOES help me a lot. I used to argue with someone with some topics and forget that I have much more important things to do. I learned from that, now I just go with "yeah ok bro you're right" or just stop saying anything and do something else. That really helps.
