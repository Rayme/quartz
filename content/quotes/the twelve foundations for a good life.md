[Scott Young](https://www.scotthyoung.com/blog/2024/07/09/the-twelve-foundations-for-a-good-life/)

They are:

- **Physical Fitness**
- Productivity
- Money
- Food
- **Reading**
- Outreach
- **Sleep**
- Reflection
- Connection
- **Focus**
- Organization
- Service

I read through all above, found that I do some of them (physical fitness, reading, reflection, organization, service) and neglect others or not dedicating to them (productivity, money, food, outreach, sleep, connection, focus).

Those matter most to me currently (back then) are: productivity, money, sleep, organization. Should try to do better on these topics, especially sleep. Others don't work if I don't sleep well. I just sleep too little these years.

Next is money. It's a bit of a personal issue. Then comes focus and organization. These two matter because I needed to do organizing a very long time ago. When I focus, I study and work well.

The last of them are connection and reflection. I don't want them to be a burden to me. They are important, but some other things matter more to me.

2025-04-03 updated:

If I am to choose the top 3 that matter to me the most in my whole lifetime, they would be:

- Physical Fitness
- Focus
- Service

For physical fitness so that I can focus, when I focus I contribute, then I serve.
