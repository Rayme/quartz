I used to buy some cheap headphones. My first nice one was from my dad, it was a Bose QC something, maybe QC25 with 3.5mm headphone jack. It was large, wonderful and quite expensive even now. It has noise-cancelling function. Everytime when I turned it on, the world became silent all of a sudden. I had an amazing experience using it.

My dad also gave me an iPod Classic 3. I love it so much. I would've wanted it to last forever. It is the best music player ever. I connect it with the QC25, had listened to a lot of music. I really enjoyed them.

Now almost every pair of expensive headphones have noise-cancelling function, even wireless ones. But I don't quite need it. The next nice one was from a good friend of mine, Sony WI-1000XM3. It supports both 3.5mm and Bluetooth. It was much smaller and compact than the Bose one, but it looked like a necklace when wearing it. It was nice but weird, I didn't use it often.

I use OnePlus Buds Pro 2 now. I use it only because I used to use Oneplus smartphones (Now I use vivo X200 Pro). If it were iPhone, my wireless headphones would've been AirPods. It's not cheap nor too expensive, it's ok. But I still can not get used to this "buds" design. Headphones should've been wired, or at least look like headphones like the old Bose.

When the AirPods released, every new little headphones look like it. Just like the day iPhone released, every smartphone looks like iPhone. It has been almost two decades already. By the way, my iPod Classic cannot connect to modern bluetooth wireless headphones. I still keep 2 or 3 wired headphones, they cost from 100 yuan to 600 yuan. Oh I miss the old times.
