---
title: Running Out of Ideas
created: 2025-11-13
---
I started this project with the goal of writing and learning English daily. Now I have written 27 tiny essays, most of them are nonsense (well, not really), because I wrote them without much organization.

The project's goal is to write 100 essays in 100 days, regardless of the quality. The first few are garbage, but they were a necessary start. Since then, I tried to write each essay with my own valuable thought in it. Through all the titles, I can see what I was thinking in those days. Now I am running out of ideas, including those drafts that I've already written in advance. I have to write one right in Bearblog now.

Here comes one idea. About writing, there are hundreds of articles I have read that encourage people to write more to clear their thinking and memorize history. I adhere to this principle and have written thousands of pieces in diaries, blog posts, daily journals, digital garden notes, and this platform Bearblog. Ideas are everywhere. I am sometimes able to convert one simple idea into a complete essay, a process that often takes hours or even days.

I am so glad that I haven't stopped writing, and trying to make it meaningful. I'll search online to see if anyone has talked about me or my work. Every time I saw something like that, I felt honored and flattered. I have even received emails that talk about my digital garden. Each of them gave me a lot of motivation.

Therefore, I will continue to write and contribute to the best of my capacity.

Day 28.
