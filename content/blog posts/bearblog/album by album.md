Today is the first day of December, it's a great day to listen to _Back To December_ by Taylor Swift.

There are some different patterns of listening to music. My favorite is listening to music album by album, from track 1 to the end of the album. It often takes about 40 minutes to 90 minutes to finish a whole album.

I developed this habit when I was young, I went to music store and bought CDs, went home and placed it into a CD player, plugged my headphones in, and started to listen. Those were times I was listening to the first 3 or 4 albums of Taylor Swift, Hybrid Theory and Meteora of Linkin Park, some greatest hits of Whitney Houston (R.I.P.) and Mariah Carey etc.

This habit follows me until today, even when I use music streaming services like Spotify or Apple Music. I would download the whole album and listen in order when I am driving or doing some light tasks. This feels great because I can understand what a singer/artist is trying to tell the audience.

The whole album is a concept. If not listening from start to finish, it would somehow break the concept, especially if the album is built on a strong notion.

But it's not absolute, I may listen to one single song repeatedly. Different patterns bring different experience. Each of them is great and choose them based on my mood.
