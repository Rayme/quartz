It's nice to keep on doing something I like.

I started to learn French on Duolingo since 2023, now it's almost 1000 days. I can never imagine time flies this fast. And it's good to know I am getting slightly better at French, even my English.

There are some other things I keep on doing for years. I have been [writing a blog](https://imzm.im/) and taking photos. I don't write many, only about 2 to 3 posts a month. But I always feel calm and peace after finishing one. It also feels great when people say my work is nice (even they aren't). So does the [digital garden](https://jefftay.com/). I don't feel shame to show them anymore.

I take a lot of photos on my smartphone. I never counted them but I guess it's almost 6-12 per day. Sometimes I take 50 photos a day and sometimes I take none. I love it when I've taken a wonderful photo. I [build a site](https://photo.imzm.org/) to exhibit them. It might be 10 years since I consider this activity seriously. I have been watching good photos and learning about photographing.

I read too but I don't really think I read that much. This has to be improved. To read more is to be better at creating more.
